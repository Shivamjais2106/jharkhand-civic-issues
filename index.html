Jharkhand Map

Jharkhand ("The land of forest") is a State in eastern India, created on 15 November 2000, from what was previously the southern half of Bihar. The State shares its border with Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha and West Bengal. It has an area of 79,714 km¬≤ (30,778 sq mi), making it the 15th largest State by area, and the 14th largest by population. Hindi is the official language. Ranchi is its capital and Dumka is the sub capital. Jharkhand is known for waterfalls, hills and holy places like Baidyanath Dham, Parasnath and Rajrappa.
